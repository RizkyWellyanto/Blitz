About us & terms and conditions

Blitz is a project made by a few college students aimed to improve the lives of others by providing an open platform for critiquing, discussing ideas and exchanging strategies for writing English essays and to preform well in literature classes. We strive to stimulate our fellow classmates’ motivation to write better essays and to practice the art of critical thinking.

2. Respect
We ask our fellow classmates to respect each other’s work. We expect all users to behave as well-mannered, contributive and collective individuals in the discussion forums. Hate speech will not be tolerated and such individuals will be permanently banned from our website.

No refunds can be made once the transaction is completed. Users are not authorized to download, save or copy paid essays by any means.
